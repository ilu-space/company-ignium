FAQ

Common questions

How quickly will I see results?

Most clients see meaningful engagement lifts within the first 30 days. Significant follower growth and reach expansion typically compounds from month 2–3 onward as the algorithm learns your content patterns.

Is this real, organic growth?

100%. We never use bots, fake followers, or engagement pods. Every follower and every like is earned through high-quality content and algorithmic positioning. This means it's sustainable and safe for your account.

Do you work with brands outside Finland?

Yes. While we're based in Finland and have deep expertise in the Nordic market, we work with ambitious brands across Europe and beyond. Instagram's algorithm is global — our strategies travel well.

Do I need to produce the content myself?

It depends on your plan. On Growth and Scale, we handle creative briefs, scripts, captions, and strategy. For video/photo assets, we collaborate with you or your team — we make the process as hands-off as possible.

Is there a minimum contract length?

No lock-in. We work month-to-month because we believe results should keep you around — not contracts. We do recommend committing to at least 90 days to see the full compounding effect of the strategy.
