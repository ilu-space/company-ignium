FAQ

Questions we get asked

Do you use bots or artificial followers?
Never. Every follower we attract is a real person who found your content genuinely interesting. We engineer organic growth through strategy and content quality — not shortcuts that get accounts banned.
How quickly will I see results?
Most clients see measurable improvements in reach and engagement within the first 30 days. Significant follower growth typically kicks in around the 60-day mark as the algorithm starts recognising your account's momentum.
Do you serve brands outside Finland?
Yes. While we're based in Finland and have deep expertise in the Nordic market, we work with ambitious brands across Europe and beyond. Our AI systems work wherever Instagram does.
What do you need from us to get started?
Instagram access (via Meta Business Suite — we never ask for passwords), your brand guidelines, and a brief kickoff call. After that, we handle the strategy and execution while you focus on your business.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. There are no long-term contracts. We operate on a monthly basis and ask for 30 days' notice to wind down cleanly — but we're confident you won't want to.
