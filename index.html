FAQ

Real questions, straight answers

What makes Ignium different from a regular social media agency? +
Most social media agencies produce content on gut feeling or trend-chasing with no systematic data behind it. Ignium uses AI-driven trend analysis to understand exactly why specific posts are performing in your niche right now — and builds content to replicate those mechanics intentionally, not accidentally.
Do you use bots, follow/unfollow tactics, or paid fake followers? +
Never. Our growth comes entirely from organic reach driven by content quality, algorithmic positioning, and posting optimization. Follower quality matters — a large audience that doesn't buy or engage is worthless to your business.
How quickly can we expect to see results? +
Organic Instagram growth through content strategy is not overnight. In the first month, we build the foundation — audit, strategy, content system, posting calendar. Meaningful growth in reach and engagement typically becomes measurable within 6–10 weeks of consistent execution. We're honest about this from day one.
Do we need to provide the actual video footage, or do you handle everything? +
This depends on your content type and is discussed in the discovery call. For many clients we handle scripting, direction, captions, and posting strategy while you or a local videographer capture raw footage. For brands that want full production we can discuss those arrangements too.
We're a small brand — are we too small for Ignium? +
Not at all. Our typical clients are e-commerce brands, personal brands, and growing businesses that are past the very early stage but don't yet have the internal expertise or bandwidth to run a serious Instagram presence. You don't need a large existing audience to start — you need a product or service worth talking about.
Do you work with brands outside the Nordics? +
Yes. While we're based in Finland and have a strong understanding of Nordic brand communication, our AI systems and content methodology work for any English or Nordic-language Instagram account globally. We work with clients across Europe and beyond.
What does pricing look like? +
We work on a monthly retainer model scoped to each client's content needs, posting frequency, and strategic complexity. We discuss this openly in the discovery call — there are no standardised packages on the website because we'd rather scope work honestly than sell you something you don't need.
